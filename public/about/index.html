About

I am a statistician and R programmer working as Associate Professor at the Center for Lifespan Changes in Brain and Cognition (LCBC) at the University of Oslo.

I did my PhD in Biostatistics at the University of Oslo with Magne Thoresen and Arnoldo Frigessi. Prior to joining LCBC, I worked for 3.5 years as an industry data scientist, the last two years at NextBridge Analytics. An up-to-date list of my publications are available at my Google Scholar Profile, and my R packages can be found at my GitHub Page.
